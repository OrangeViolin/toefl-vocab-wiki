# freeze

> **发音**：[friːz]　|　**所在**：[[Word-List-2]]（WL02-003）

## 释义

- v.（使）结冰，（使）凝固

## 常见搭配

- freeze over 冰封；freeze up （机器、引擎等中的水）结冰

## 例句

- Jack caught a fish and dropped it beside him on the ice and it froze solid. 杰克捉到一条鱼，将其随手扔在身旁的冰上，结果鱼冻得硬邦邦的。// I was really happy to be writing a detective story. But after the first few pages, I sort of froze up mentally. 我很高兴能写侦探小说。但是只写了几页，我的脑子就不转了。

## 📖 真实例句（100句书）

> *「Most substance contract when they freeze so that the density of a substance’s solid is higher than the density of its liquid.」*
> 大多数物质冻结时会收缩，所以它们的密度在固态时高于液态时。
