# Sentence 28

> **Even though the precise qualities of hero in literary words may vary over time, the basic exemplary function of the hero seems to remain constant.**
>
> 即便文学语言中描述的英雄所具备的确切品质会随时代变化，但英雄基本的典范作用似乎是保持不变的。

## 语法笔记
