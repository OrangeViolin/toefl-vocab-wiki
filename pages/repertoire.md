# repertoire

> **发音**：[ˈrepərtwɑːr]　|　**所在**：[[Word-List-34]]（WL34-035）

## 释义

- n.（剧团等的）常备剧目，保留剧目；（剧团、演员等的）可表演节目；全部才能

## 记忆方法

词根记忆：re（再，又）＋pert（带）＋oire→反复带着的剧目→（剧团等）的常备剧目

## 例句

- All teachers have a variety of techniques and activities that they regularly use — their repertoire. 所有老师都有着多种常用的讲课技巧和课堂活动，这是他们的常备技能。

## 📖 真实例句（100句书）

> *「Thus, the recalling of an event （a hunt, battle, or other feat） is elaborated through the narrator’s pantomime and impersonation and eventually through each role being assumed by a different person.」*
