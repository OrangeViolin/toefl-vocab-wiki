# inflation

> **发音**：[ɪnˈfleɪʃn]　|　**所在**：[[Word-List-10]]（WL10-002）

## 释义

- n. 通货膨胀，物价上涨；（充气而引起的）膨胀

## 例句

- The governments in some countries have taken measures to control the inflation rate. 一些国家的政府已经采取了措施来控制通货膨胀率。

## 📖 真实例句（100句书）

> *「The elimination of inflation would ensure that the amount of money used in repaying a loan would have the same value as the amount of money borrowed.」*
> 消除通货膨胀，可确保用来偿还贷款的钱与借贷的钱价值相等。
