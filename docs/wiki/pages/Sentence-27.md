# Sentence 27

> **The application of electronic controls made possible by the microprocessor and computer storage have multiplied the uses of the modern typewriter.**
>
> 借助微处理器和电脑存储，电子控制装置的运用成为可能，该运用成倍地增加了现代打字机的功能。

## 语法笔记

本句的主干是The application of electronic controls have multiplied the uses of the modern typewriter。过去分词短语made possible by the microprocessor and computer storage作后置定语修饰electronic controls。
