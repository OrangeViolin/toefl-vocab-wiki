# Sentence 1

> **Essentially, a theory is an abstract, symbolic representation of what is conceived to be reality.**
>
> 理论在本质上是对人们构想的现实的一种抽象和符号化的表达。

## 语法笔记

本句的主干是“A theory is an abstract, symbolic representation”。what引导的一个名词性从句充当of的宾语。
