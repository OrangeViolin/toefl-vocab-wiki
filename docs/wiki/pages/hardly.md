# hardly

> **发音**：[ˈhɑːrdli]　|　**所在**：[[Word-List-22]]（WL22-038）

## 释义

- ad. 几乎不，简直不；刚刚，仅仅（barely, scarcely）

## 例句

- It was hardly worth the price of admission. 花钱买那张电影票都不值。// I hardly ever use the dictionary anyway. 反正我几乎不用字典。
